# failure-source-not-prioritized

## Trigger
- Cheap source surfaces or actual source access existed, but the solve stayed phenomenon-first.

## Why it looked reasonable
- Black-box probes were easy to continue and kept producing some noise.

## Misleading signal
- Repeated response differences were treated as stronger than available source.

## Earlier kill signal
- Source acquisition cost was lower than continuing branch complexity.

## Better next probe
- Recover source first, then run backward slice to sink/flag/config/secret/admin routes.

## Stop rule
- Do not deepen complex medium-value branches while a cheap source/data surface is still untested.

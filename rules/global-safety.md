# Global Safety Rules

Use these rules for every challenge:

- Work only on authorized CTF, lab, benchmark, or explicit practice targets.
- Do not attack unrelated third-party infrastructure.
- Do not guess flags or treat hidden evaluator metadata as the answer.
- Prefer low-cost triage before exploitation.
- Before destructive, repeated, or high-concurrency actions, record a risk plan and a canary.
- Preserve original artifacts for forensics and reverse engineering work.
